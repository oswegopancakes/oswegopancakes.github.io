The Oswego Kiwanis Chapter is partnering with the Oswego Police Department & Explorer Club Post 3525 for Our 2020 Pancake Breakfast! Proceeds benefit the Oswego Police Explorer Post, Kiwanis of Oswego, and Concerns of Police Survivors (COPS).

Join us on February 29, 2020 from 8AM-12PM for all the Pancakes you can eat at Oswego High School. After Breakfast stay for the Oswego Hometown Expo located in the Oswego High School Field House from 9AM-2PM - Free Admission!
